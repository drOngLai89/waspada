Waspada Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 Dec 2025

Waspada is a scam-awareness and safety app. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

What Waspada handles

  • Images you choose to submit for analysis (for example screenshots). The image is sent to our servers to generate an analysis result and report.
  • Basic app/device information (such as app version and error details) may be processed to keep the service running and to troubleshoot issues.

How we use this information

  • To provide the analysis feature and generate a result/report.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

Sharing

  • Information may be processed by service providers used to run the app (for example cloud hosting and analysis services). It is used only to deliver the feature and operate the service.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Retention

We aim to keep submitted images only as long as needed to provide the analysis and for short-term troubleshooting, then delete them.

Permissions

  • Camera (if enabled): Used only to capture an image you choose to analyse.
  • Photos/Media access: Used only to select an image you choose to analyse.

Children

Waspada is not intended for children under 13.
